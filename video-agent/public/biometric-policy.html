Biometric Data Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

This policy describes how Voxaris AI ("we", "us") collects, uses, stores, and destroys biometric data during AI-powered video interviews and virtual property showings conducted through our platform.

1. What We Collect

During a live video session, our AI perception system analyzes the following in real time:

  • Facial expression signals — engagement level, emotional state (e.g. enthusiasm, hesitation, confusion), and attention direction (gaze tracking).
  • Voice characteristics — speech pace, tone, confidence level, and decisiveness signals.

These signals are processed as transient numerical features within the live session. We do not create persistent biometric templates, faceprints, or voiceprints that could be used to identify you outside of the session.

2. Purpose

The sole purpose of this analysis is to help the AI agent adapt its conversational approach during the session — for example, pausing when you seem to be thinking, or exploring a topic further when you show strong interest. The perception data is also summarized in a post-session report delivered to a human recruiter or listing agent to provide context about the conversation flow.

3. Consent

We collect biometric data only after you provide explicit written consent by checking the "Biometric Data" checkbox on the pre-interview form. You may decline consent, in which case you can request a human-led alternative interview or showing by contacting accommodations@voxaris.io.

4. Retention and Destruction

Retention Schedule

Real-time perception signals (facial expression scores, voice characteristics) are held in volatile memory only during the live session and are discarded when the session ends. They are never written to persistent storage.

Session Recordings

Video and audio recordings of the session are stored for a maximum of 90 days from the session date, or until the initial purpose (recruiter review, listing agent follow-up) has been satisfied — whichever comes first. After that period, recordings are permanently deleted from all storage systems.

Destruction on Request

You may request deletion of all data associated with your session at any time by emailing privacy@voxaris.io with your name and the approximate date of your session. We will confirm destruction within 30 days of receiving your request.

5. Disclosure and Sharing

Biometric data is not sold, leased, or traded to any third party. Perception summaries (not raw biometric data) may be shared with:

  • The staffing agency or brokerage that initiated the session (your prospective employer or listing agent).
  • Tavus Inc. — our CVI infrastructure provider — which processes perception signals on our behalf under a data processing agreement.

6. Applicable Law

This policy is designed to comply with the Illinois Biometric Information Privacy Act (740 ILCS 14), Maryland HB 1202 (MD Labor § 3-717), the Illinois Artificial Intelligence Video Interview Act (820 ILCS 42), and other applicable state biometric privacy statutes. If there is a conflict between this policy and applicable law, the law controls.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy: privacy@voxaris.io
