03 · An exchange, from a sample tape

Sandra Mills, F&I.

FILE · Mills_S_F&I_2026-03-04.txt Q 07 OF 15 · 11:32 AM
Jordan
You've spent eight years selling F&I products into one of the worst markets dealers have seen. Walk me through a deal you almost lost — and what you said in the box that turned it around.
Sandra Mills
Last August. Couple in their sixties, cash buyer on a used Tahoe. The default move is to skip the F&I pitch entirely — they're paying cash, why bother. I asked them, instead, what would happen if the transmission went next March. They didn't have an answer. By the time we walked through the GAP and the powertrain warranty against their actual driving pattern — Florida, towing a boat — they took both. Three thousand back of the house on a deal we almost stamped paid-in-full and walked.
Jordan
What's the one thing you'd want a finance director to know about you in their first week?
Sandra Mills
That I will not lie to a customer to clear a deal. I have walked away from three months of a board because the manager wanted me to bury a fee. If you need that, hire someone else.
